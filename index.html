About me

Video editor &
post-production specialist

I'm a video editor with over 3 years of experience at Render Producción Audiovisual, a Bogotá-based production house with presence in 10 Latin American countries. I've worked on post-production for TV commercials, product spots, motion graphics, corporate reels, aerial footage and music videos for leading brands like Quala, Alpina, Colombina and more.

My toolkit centers on Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve — covering everything from assembly and fine cuts to color correction, audio mixing and delivery in broadcast and digital formats.
